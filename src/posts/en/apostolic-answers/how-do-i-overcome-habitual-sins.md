<p>What do I do if I keep falling in the same sin over and over and over again? What do I do if I've been confessing it for years on end and it's always the same cycle of falling into the same habits? First and foremost, do not give in to despair. Recognize that what the devil wants to do is to convince you that there's no hope, that you won't be able to beat this habit, this sin.</p>
<br>
<p>And there's a little bit of a truth in that. You won't be able to. But with the help of the Holy Spirit and the grace of God, we can overcome every sin. We can be more than conquerors in Christ who sets us free. But the idea is to turn to God and to recognize with humility that we are completely dependent on His grace.</p>
<br>
<p>And regardless of how long it takes, regardless if I ever have to struggle with this sin for the rest of my life, as long as I hold on to Christ and as long as I continue to do my part, then the Holy Spirit can work in me and help set me free from that sin.</p>
