<p>And what if a person falls for the same sin again and again, even after repeated confession? What advice shall we give him?</p>
<br>
<p>You keep getting up, like, you keep getting up and don't worry about how many times you fall. I really mean that. We have to stop counting how many times we fell as if somehow God is sitting there, you know what I'm saying, you know: If you do it one more time... You know, all you have is six more chances. If... if you... if you take up those six chances, it's over for you!</p>
<br>
<p>That's not the way God deals with us. That's not the way the Lord works and that's not the God who was revealed to us. So, I wanna encourage you: don't worry about how often you fall; just make sure you keep getting up.</p>
<br>
<p>I mean, this is not permission for you to fall; this is me encouraging you to struggle. But if your struggle leads to you falling, it's perfectly fine.</p>
