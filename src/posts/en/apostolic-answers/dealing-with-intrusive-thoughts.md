<p>What is one of the ways that I can fight off intrusive thoughts? What do I mean by intrusive thoughts? Think of those thoughts that maybe bring you to a place where you feel like your mind and your heart are darkened. There's like a dark cloud over your mind. Think about those thoughts that oftentimes force themselves in and then you find yourself having to deal with a tremendous amount of despair, maybe shame, maybe guilt.</p>
<br>
<p>What are one of the ways that the Church introduces to you and me, so that we can fight off those thoughts? Well, the Church actually encourages you and me to place our mind on higher things, and more specifically, by calling on the name of the Lord Jesus Christ. In the Coptic Orthodox tradition, there are ψάλεις (psalis) or very specific praises that we pray every single day that are dedicated to the Lord Jesus Christ. But the mere and very simple and focused Jesus Prayer is a great asset:</p>
<br>
<p>Lord Jesus Christ, Son of the living God, have mercy on me, a sinner.</p>
<br>
<p>Next time you're having an intrusive thought, call on the Lord through this prayer and see how quickly He sends grace.</p>
