<p>Does Christ intercede? Of course He intercedes! Of course He intercedes! What we have to understand as far as intercession is when He prays on our behalf, when He stands before the Father and He says: They are yours and I want them to be with Us.</p>
<br>
<p>Remember that this is precisely what He did even on the cross when He says: Forgive them, Father, for they know not what they do. That's a beautiful example of intercession, where He stands there as the mediator, where He, as both perfect God and perfect human being, is capable of mediating between us and God the Father.</p>
<br>
<p>And so, yes, it's absolutely normal for us to speak of the intercession of the Son. It's absolutely normal for us to speak also of the intercession of the Holy Spirit. Intercession literally here means that they stand for us and speak for us.</p>
