<p>Just yesterday, many of us in the entire world was horrified at seeing the scene of a man attempting to take the life of Bishop Mar Mari Emmanuel of the Assyrian Church of the East. And to say the least, many of us were scandalized by how it is that we witnessed a man being on the receiving end of so much hatred and so much violence.</p>
<br>
<p>And while many of us are now called to pray for the safe recovery and the well-being of Bishop Mar Mari Emmanuel, we also need to take a moment to be able to thank him; to thank him, because his response to that hatred was to pray and to bless and to encourage everyone to pray with him for the person who committed the act.</p>
