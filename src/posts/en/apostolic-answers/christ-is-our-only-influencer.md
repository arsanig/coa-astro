<p>Quick message to all of our young people who are into following influencers... Just a quick question: What are we doing here? What is it about these people that you feel is so admirable? Forgive me but, at some point, you gotta ask yourself the question: Are these people actually living up to the Gospel? Is there anything that you can actually learn from their values and their principles?</p>
<br>
<p>Oftentimes, we're following people simply because they're famous. We're following people because other people are following them. What is it about Kim Kardashian? What is it about Taylor Swift? What is it about Andrew Tate that is so intriguing to us? What is it about people like Kendrick Lamar and Drake who are fighting it out publicly and doing whatever it is that they're doing?</p>
<br>
<p>We should have Christ as our only influencer. The only person worth following and to keep your eyes on and to allow His life to be the perfect model of how you should live is our Lord Jesus Christ. He should be the only influencer.</p>
